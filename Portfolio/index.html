About

I'm Chloe and these are the inventions I like to display! There are tons of others that don't make it here..

I call my projects Inventions-

    In these inventions, I try to include:
  • Starting point: What was I trying to achieve or solve? Sometimes it was just for the aesthetics.
  • The perfect vision I had in mind: A silly habit of imagining something perfect, and have it crash with the imperfection of the material.
  • Materials & Tools Used: Specific tools, software, platforms etc. I always want these to be free or FOSS
  • Process: An overview of how it was done, best with step-by-step
  • Results: Did it work? What were the final results?
  • To Improve: What would I do differently next time?

For older inventions-- which I wouldn't really remember the methods-- I write an article with mostly my feelings about an invention.
