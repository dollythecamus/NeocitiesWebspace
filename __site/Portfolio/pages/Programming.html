Gatos e Bombas (2025)

  • Godot, GDScript
  • Digital Card Game

Tasked with making a digital card game at university, my team and I worked on this, and I was the main programmer, working on the mechanics of card movement; game rules and card effects; the bot that the player is playing against; and the visual feedbacks that make the game easier to play.


It's turn based and quick, the cats play the cards they pulled at the start, if they pull a bomb card and fail to take it off their hands, they lose.
